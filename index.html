   How To Use?


  1. Left Panel - Calculator and Right Panel - List of Wild Pokemon & Trainers Exp.

  2. Type your pokemon's current experience. Note: Target Exp is already assigned as 100,000 value.

  3. It will shows solution button. Battle only pokemon and trainer(s) which generated by the calculator.

  4. If no possible solution generate, try to battle wild pokemon in map. (It's better to calculate first before you get your ideal experience).

  5. You can click the trainer and pokemon in right panel. It will shows a popover that will redirect to the trainers and map for wild pokemon.

  6. If you want other EXP Calculator for DelugeRPG - visit Psyduck Exp Calculator and Tolga's Exp Calculator (Outdated).
NOTE: Your pokemon must be Level 99 and the current exp must be minimum of 99,000 and maximum of 99,977.
You can battle any other wild pokemon/trainers that give the same amount of exp as those in the suggested solution.
